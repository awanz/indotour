Top 8 Travel

Indonesia has many beautiful places to visit, of the many places we have travel recommendations while in Indonesia.

Jakarta
Jakarta is the center of the capital city of Indonesia, where many community activities are active here.
Bandung
Bandung is the capital city of West Java province as well as a metropolitan city. The city is located 140 km southeast of Jakarta, and is the third largest city in Indonesia after Jakarta and Surabaya by population.
Yogyakarta
Yogyakarta is a special region within the Unitary State of the Republic of Indonesia which still maintains a sultanate-shaped government in its regional administration.
Surabaya
The city of Surabaya is the capital of the province of East Java, Indonesia. Surabaya is the second largest city in Indonesia after Jakarta. Surabaya is the center of business, trade, industry and education in the eastern region of Java Island and its surroundings.
Bali
Bali is an island in Indonesia known for its green volcanic mountains, unique rice terraces, beaches, and beautiful coral reefs. There are many religious tourism spots such as Uluwatu Temple which stands on a cliff.
Nusa Tenggara Barat
Nusa Tenggara Barat is a province in Indonesia located in the western part of the Nusa Tenggara Islands. This province has a capital city in Mataram and has 10 districts and 2 cities, including the city of Mataram.
Tana Toraja
Tana Toraja Regency is one of the districts in South Sulawesi Province, with a regent named Ir. Nico Biringkanae. The capital of this district is Makale.
Papua
Papua is a province located in the central part of Papua Island or the easternmost part of Indonesia's Papua territory. Its eastern hemisphere is the country of Papua New Guinea. The province of Papua was previously named Irian Jaya which covers the entire territory of Papua Island.
